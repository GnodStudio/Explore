Welcome to This Game:

Cube's Explore is a game about a cube who was trapped in a mysterious place.

You must help him to find a way out.

How to Play:

Using virtual key left(←) and right(→) on the screen bottom left to control the cube's moving.

Using virtual key up(↑) on the screen bottom right to control the cube's jumping

Tips:

You can die countless times.

When you die,There might have chance to show AD（Well, the poor author is going to live on this).
